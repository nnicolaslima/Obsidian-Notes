
> "These are the lands from old, the lands beyond the sea, the lands of the forgotten and the home of the untold."

As far as the tales go as to the origins of these lands...they say there was a man, he who was a conqueror, Ugarth, the Immortal, he had four sons, which are branches of the same tree. Yet, each son would take a tree of his own, as a mark of their territory.
